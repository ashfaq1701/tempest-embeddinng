RESEARCH (web, grounded) — TPNet mechanism = Temporal Walk Matrix Projection
  arXiv 2410.04013 (NeurIPS 2024). Fetched, not from memory.
================================================================================
  - Temporal walk matrix: A_{u,w}^(i)(t) = Σ_{W: length-i temporal walks u->w} s(W),
    s(W)=Π e^{-λ(t - t_i)}. = time-decayed COUNT of MULTI-HOP temporal walks between
    u and w, per hop length i (all lengths l=0..k).
  - Node reps H_u^(l)(t) maintained by RANDOM FEATURE PROPAGATION: on edge (u,v,t),
    H_u^(l)(t+) = H_u^(l)(t) + e^{λt} H_v^(l-1)(t). Theorem: e^{-λlt}H^(l)=A^(l)(t)P
    (walk matrix x random projection), so <H_u^(l1),H_v^(l2)> ~= <A_u^(l1),A_v^(l2)>
    = count of length-(l1+l2) temporal walks connecting u,v. O(k·d_r) per edge.
  - Pairwise link feature: Gram matrix of the two nodes' multi-hop reps,
    f_{u,v}=MLP(log(ReLU(flat(F_u,v F_u,v^T)+1))). I.e. INNER PRODUCTS of multi-hop
    temporal-walk-count vectors = dense multi-hop co-reachability.
  - INDUCTIVE: TPNet is transductive; does NOT handle truly unseen nodes. => matches
    our finding that new x u-only-inductive has CN=0 (hard wall for BOTH). TPNet's
    win is on new x BOTH-seen (connected via multi-hop walks, no direct edge).

  IMPLICATIONS for our gap:
  - The signal that wins new-pairs is DENSE MULTI-HOP temporal-walk-COUNT between u,v,
    captured EXACTLY via random-projection propagation (the FULL walk matrix), not a
    few SAMPLED short walks. Our 5x5 sampled bags are sparse+shallow -> 15.5% coverage.
    This explains the coverage cap precisely.
  - Two routes to close it (the round-2 fork):
    (a) DEEPEN/WIDEN our sampled walks so CN coverage rises toward the walk matrix —
        cheap, walks-only/in-spirit, but sampling may plateau below dense propagation.
        (depth sweep in progress tests exactly this.)
    (b) ADD a random-feature-propagation reachability channel (TPNet's core): maintain
        per-node multi-hop walk-count random-projection states, use <H_u,H_v> (Gram)
        as a co-reach feature, fused with the geometric head. Transformative but the
        PROVEN dense mechanism; not a hand-crafted pair store (a model state, causal,
        cheap O(k·d_r)/edge). Arguably the right "massive change" the user invited.
  - Decision data for (a) vs (b) = the depth sweep (does sampled coverage rise enough?).
    Round 2 convenes managers with: 5x5 diagnostic + depth sweep + this TPNet grounding.
